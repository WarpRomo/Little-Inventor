It starts from an imagination...

HeartForce

Built by the Little Inventor founders, a device that helps astronaut survive rocket launches by detecting irregularities in heart rate due to atmospheric force, and vehicular force. Built using Arduino components sourced from the Little Inventor Charity. Provides real-time monitoring of heart rate, G-force, and oxygen saturation levels for individuals wearing the HeartForce wristband.

Little Supplies

Our resource fund supplies inventors with components, from Arduino and Raspberry Pi modules to PCBs and sensors, enabling them to bring ideas to life. This fund has already supported projects like the HeartForce wristband—a life-saving device that monitors astronauts’ heart rate, G-force, and oxygen saturation during rocket launches. We ensure that creators have access to quality materials and guidance, empowering them to push boundaries and develop impactful technology.
