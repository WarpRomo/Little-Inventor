Why did we create Little Inventor?

The Little Inventor Charity was born out of a simple idea: to empower the next generation of creators, dreamers, and innovators by providing them the support they need to bring their ideas to life. Our founders, Ritik, Aryen, and Jayven, all share a passion for technology, creativity, and problem-solving. They noticed a gap—an absence of accessible resources for young inventors to explore their ideas, especially those without the means or support to get started.

What began as a personal mission for each founder, soon became a shared vision. Ritik’s love for Arduino and programming fueled his drive to help young creators understand the magic of making things with their own hands. Aryen’s expertise in data science revealed the potential for young minds to harness data to solve real-world problems. Jayven’s work in public relations and sponsorships highlighted the need for connections and mentorship in bringing ideas to market. Together, they realized that by joining forces, they could not only provide resources but also a community for budding inventors.

Today, Little Inventor stands as a haven for aspiring minds, a place where everyone can find the support, knowledge, and encouragement to bring their unique visions into the world. Every step of the way, from securing funding to accessing high-quality materials and experienced mentorship, we are dedicated to helping our participants reach their fullest potential. Our mission is simple: to ensure no idea is left unexplored and every young inventor has the tools they need to make a difference.
